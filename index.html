Welcome

I built my first computer when I was 13 and started to learn about programming in High School. I never pursued a career in development, opting instead to study video game design at Savannah College of Art and Design to focus on my artistic muscles. Now I’ve decided to go back to my roots and combine the passion I have for programming with the creativity I’ve fostered in my years practicing design.
